My Story.

Dr. Alves has 10+ years of experience with robotic hardware and software, and 2 years with virtual reality and simulation. He managed several R&D projects, and mentored multidisciplinary teams of graduate and undergraduate students. He applies his extensive experience with human-centered design and technological know-how to help organizations and their clients to reach their goals.

More About Me.

I am the Research Lead of the 3Sphere Robot Project at JDQ Systems Inc., Vancouver, BC, and a Post-doctoral Fellow at the Ontario Tech University (OTU), Oshawa, ON. I am also member of the MaxSIMhealth research group at OTU.

What can I do for you.

With experience in both Academia and Industry, I can help you navigate a multidisciplinary team of designers, developers and researchers to create exciting products with real-world impact.

Socially Assistive Robots

I have plenty of experience with robotics, particularly with those whose goal is to improve the quality-of-life of seniors, people living with disabilities, and caregivers. My main area of interest is Human-Robot Interaction and Socially-Aware Control Architectures. I have solid experience with the Robot Operating System (ROS), and with the application of machine learning methods for robot perception.

Simulation and Virtual Reality

Over the past two years I have been actively working with robot simulation and Virtual Reality (VR). I believe that VR can empower users and researchers, since it can immerse people into the simulated environment and visualize a system's input and output in a clear and meaningful way that is impractical in the real world. VR also help people to impersonate different avatars in order to evaluate the robot's perception and interaction modules, which can improve the results yielded by the simulation.

Teaching and E-learning

As an educator, I adapt the philosophy of Paulo Freire. I enjoy interacting with students to learn about them and adapt the course to the context of their backgrounds and to motivate them to be proactive in achieving their personal and professional goals. I like to share my research and industry experience to inspire them and help them to take ownership of their own stories.

I have experience creating online courses for a broad audience, be it synchronous or asynchronous learning. My courses have been deployed in different platforms, including Canvas and Moodle.

Project Management and Mentorship

Throughout my career in both Academia and Industry, I have been mentoring students to help them achieve their research and professional goals. I have co-supervised graduate (Ph.D. and Master) and undergraduate students, managed Capstone projects, and provided mentoring for students during their job-interviewing process.
